ABOUT PROJECT

What is SwipeWaifu?

SwipeWaifu is an open-source anime waifu discovery app built for anime enthusiasts. Swipe through 30+ SFW categories, save your favorites, use keyboard shortcuts on desktop, and chat with the Anime Fanbase community — all in one place. Available on Android, Web, and soon on Windows & Linux.

The project aims to be fast, fun, and free — celebrating anime culture through clean design and a constantly growing image library. Open source and community-driven, contributions are always welcome.

PRIVACY POLICY

TL;DR SwipeWaifu does not collect, store, or sell your personal data. Ever.

No Data Collection

SwipeWaifu does not collect any personal information. No accounts, no sign-ups, no tracking. You use the app anonymously.

Images via waifu.pics

All images are fetched from the public waifu.pics API. We do not host or store any images ourselves. Their API is stateless — no user data is sent.

Local Storage Only

Your saved favorites and preferences (dark/light theme, etc.) are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Community Chat

The built-in Anime Fanbase community chat is a third-party integration. Messages posted there are subject to that platform's own privacy policy. Please be mindful of what you share publicly.

No Cookies or Ads

SwipeWaifu uses no advertising cookies, no analytics trackers, and no third-party ad networks. The docs site uses localStorage only to remember your theme preference.

Contact

Have a privacy concern or question? Open an issue on GitHub and we'll respond as soon as possible.
